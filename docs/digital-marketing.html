Digital Marketing

Below are selected projects that reflect my experience in digital marketing strategy, campaign analysis, SEO research, and data-driven decision making. These projects combine creative marketing ideas with analytics tools to better understand customer behavior, improve engagement, and support business goals.

ODI Grips AI Caption Strategy Project

For this graduate research project, I analyzed how AI-generated social media captions performed under different prompting strategies. Using historical Instagram data from ODI Grips, I evaluated how structured prompts influenced caption quality, engagement potential, and brand alignment.

The project combined marketing strategy with analytics by comparing caption performance patterns, testing audience engagement metrics, and presenting findings through visual reports and statistical analysis.

Skills Demonstrated:

  • Content Strategy: Evaluated caption structure, tone, and audience engagement.
  • Marketing Analytics: Analyzed Instagram performance metrics using R.
  • Consumer Engagement Research: Studied how prompt structure influenced perceived quality.
  • Data Visualization: Built visual reports and presentation-ready findings in Quarto.

Boiling Point Digital Marketing Strategy Analysis

For this project, I analyzed Boiling Point’s digital marketing strategy and how the brand adapts to changing customer behavior within the fast-casual restaurant industry. The project explored how influencer partnerships, short-form video content, social media engagement, and promotional campaigns contribute to brand visibility and customer engagement.

The analysis also examined the shift toward digital-first marketing, emphasizing how authenticity, emotional storytelling, and creator-driven content have become increasingly important in driving engagement and customer connection.

Skills Demonstrated:

  • Digital Marketing Strategy: Evaluated how Boiling Point uses digital channels to support customer engagement and brand growth.
  • Influencer Marketing Analysis: Examined creator partnerships, promotional campaigns, and engagement effectiveness.
  • Consumer Engagement Research: Analyzed how customer behavior and content preferences influence marketing performance.
  • Content Strategy: Explored how authentic and visually engaging content supports social media visibility and audience interaction.
  • Marketing Communication: Presented findings through structured analysis, visual examples, and strategic recommendations.
